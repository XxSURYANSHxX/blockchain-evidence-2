Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

Blockchain Data

  • Wallet Addresses: Your MetaMask wallet address for authentication
  • Evidence Hashes: Cryptographic hashes of evidence files (NOT the actual files)
  • Transaction Records: Blockchain transaction hashes for audit trails
  • Timestamps: When evidence was submitted or accessed

Application Data

  • User Profile: Name, role, department, jurisdiction
  • Case Information: Case numbers, titles, descriptions
  • Activity Logs: System access and action records
  • Evidence Metadata: File names, types, sizes (stored securely off-chain)

2. What Goes On-Chain vs Off-Chain

On Blockchain (Public)

  • Evidence file hashes (SHA-256)
  • Wallet addresses
  • Timestamps
  • Transaction hashes

Off-Chain (Private)

  • Actual evidence files
  • Personal information
  • Case details
  • User profiles

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Evidence Management: Secure storage and verification of digital evidence
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions and authentication
  • Audit Trails: Maintaining chain of custody records
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting judicial and regulatory requirements
  • System Security: Monitoring for unauthorized access

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Law Enforcement: With authorized law enforcement agencies
  • Judicial Proceedings: As evidence in court cases
  • System Administration: With authorized system administrators

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication
  • Blockchain Security: Immutable records on distributed ledger
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade hosting and monitoring

6. Data Retention

  • Evidence Data: Retained according to legal requirements and case status
  • Blockchain Records: Permanent and immutable by design
  • User Accounts: Active until deactivated by administrator
  • Activity Logs: Retained for audit and compliance purposes

7. Your Rights

  • Access: Request information about your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion where legally permissible
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

Note: Some rights may be limited due to legal evidence preservation requirements.

8. International Compliance

  • GDPR: European Union General Data Protection Regulation
  • DPDP Act: India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act
  • Local Laws: Compliance with applicable regional regulations

9. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: DGC2MHNE@proton.me
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Policy Inquiry"
  • Response Time: Within 30 days

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the system notification system.
